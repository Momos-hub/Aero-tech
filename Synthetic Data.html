What is Synthetic Data?

Synthetic data is artificially generated data that mimics real-world data. It is used to train machine learning models, especially when real data is scarce or sensitive. By using synthetic data, organizations can enhance their models without compromising privacy or security.

Benefits of Using Synthetic Data

  • Improved model accuracy by providing diverse training scenarios.
  • Reduced risk of data privacy issues.
  • Cost-effective data generation without the need for extensive data collection.
  • Ability to simulate rare events that may not be present in real datasets.

Applications in Aerospace Maintenance

Synthetic data can be used in predictive maintenance to simulate various failure scenarios, allowing engineers to train models that can predict potential issues before they occur. This proactive approach can significantly reduce downtime and maintenance costs.
